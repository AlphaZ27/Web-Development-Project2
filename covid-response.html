The safest space for family entertainment


Measures to have a Safe Experience:

Epic Cinemas Limited (ECL) operates several multiplexes across Trinidad and Tobago. To ensure our venues provide the safest possible entertainment experience, we have spent the sum of $6.5 million to upgrade our premises for the assurance of our customers. We want our customers to have a safe and family-friendly cinema experience.

What to expect when you visit:

Following an extended closure due to the spread of COVID-19, ECL is preparing for the restart of its operations.


Cleaning/Sanitization

The number of show times per day have been reduced with thorough cleaning conducted between show times.
Contactless NFC payment terminals have been introduced and card payment terminals are wiped clean after each use.

Physical Distancing

Alternating rows of seats between patron-occupied rows and vacant buffer rows.
Seats on either side of all patron bookings are automatically made unavailable for use.
Patrons must remain six feet apart when in line for tickets, screen entry or concessions, guided by newly installed floor markings.

Masks

All employees must wear face masks and gloves while serving patrons.
All patrons must wear masks while on premises except during the consumption of food or beverages.

Ticketing

Family bookings are permitted in groups of no more than five persons.

Bathrooms

All washroom taps, soap dispensers and flushing mechanisms have been replaced with touchless motion sensor units.

General Health and Safety

Transparent acrylic separation screens have been installed at all service counters.
Touchless hand sanitizer dispensers have been installed at all entrances and service points.
External doors have been upgraded with automatic sliding mechanisms, and all internal doors have been modified to swing both in and out with foot-pusher plates to minimize hand contact.
